t'azur Insurance Company Distributes Gifts to Salmaniya Hospital for Eid

08 September   0

Manama, Bahrain: t'azur Islamic Insurance Company distributed around 70 gifts of Sweets and Dates in hand packaged glass boxes to patients in the Oncology Ward and the Kidney Failure Ward at the Salmaniya Hospital for Eid Al Fitr. Two of t'azur's team members were on hand to unload a carload of gifts for delivery into the wards at the Salmaniya Hospital.

t'azur proudly donated both time and gifts to help patients at the Hospital celebrate Eid. This donation gives t'azur another opportunity to give back and promote the well being of children and family in the community t'azur serves.
